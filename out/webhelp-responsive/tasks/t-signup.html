How to Create an Account

Thank you for your interest in AirClassroom! Let's create your account.

You can access AirClassroom on your desktop or mobile device.

  1. Go to https://www.airclassroom.com.
  2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner.
  3. Enter your information into the appropriate fields.
    You will not be able to use the same email address for two different accounts.
  4. Confirm your email address.
  5. Optional: Upload a profile picture that shows your face.
  6. See How to Reserve a Classroom to make your first reservation.
