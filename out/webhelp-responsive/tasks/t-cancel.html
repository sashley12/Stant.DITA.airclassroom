How to Cancel a Reservation

If it turns out you can't make your reservation, here's how to cancel.

A reservation must be cancelled at least two days before the date, or a late cancellation fee will be applied to your card.

  1. Go to https://www.airclassroom.com.
  2. Click on "My Reservations" in the top, right corner.
  3. Click "Cancel Reservation" under the reservation you wish to cancel.
  4. Optional: Give us a reason for why you cancelled your reservation.
